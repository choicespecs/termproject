Short Synopsis

Hybrid Synchronous Learning

One of the biggest challenges in modern education involves tackling how to improve the synchronous hybrid classroom. This doesn't necessarily mean improvements for solely one particular group such as students, educators, or even tackling only in-class challenges and online challenges. This is a problem that encompasses several different areas and groups.

Our group has decided to focus mainly on tackling the large pedagogical issues that face hybrid synchronous classrooms mainly involving feedback and engagement which are two main issues that are problematic for instructors and students.
