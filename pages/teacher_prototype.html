User Story

How might we as students accomplish "slow down" so that the instructor reviews the content.

Explanation

The most difficult aspect of online hybrid synchronous classrooms is the lack of interactions that can occur between teachers and students. In real life classrooms, teachers can get live feedback from students. However; online classrooms make it far more difficult to receive feedback, but also receive it non-intrusively. There can be too many notifications and popups that teachers must be aware of and can distract instructors from teaching.

This design allows students to non-intrusively give direct feedback to instructors and at the same time, allows instructors to also guage both individual needs and classroom needs as well. The slow down feature in our prototype actually will darken in intensity and shade as more students request for slow down. This will help teachers understand how quick or how slow their pace may need since more slow down requests may indicate a larger issue. The raise hand feature will also allow students to cater to individual student needs and students will be allowed to give feedback and interactions without calling any attention to themselves.
