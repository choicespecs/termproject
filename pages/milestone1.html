Problem Proposal

Milestone #1

We decided to look at some pedagogical issues that face both teachers and students such as the difficulties with engagement, issues with real time feedback / assessment for both teachers and students, and providing a way to have an alternative way to provide instruction and learning outside of traditional forms (traditional forms of learning include methods such as utilizing videos, ppt, which seem to be the predominant way hybrid synchronous classes provide instruction to both in-class and online students).

There currently are platforms such as top hat, kahoot, and other websites and app that have given instructors and students ways to interact more within the online / in class environment but we will explore an alternative method or way to approach teaching and learning that can incorporate more alternative ways to teach. We have discussed the use of possibly software / application that uses survey's, quizzes, or modules andhe same time this software / web applications / etc. can help provide both students and instructors with ways to provide and obtain feedback (such as through statistical analysis, real time feedback of answers, etc.)
